We are continually expanding the information from your medical record that you can see on YourHealth Online.  You now have access to view your past medical and surgical history, as well as your current health issues.  Adolescents and their parents will not see these sections. The descriptions that you see are medical terms and may be hard to understand.  If you have questions about a diagnosis, feel that information is incorrect, or want to provide updated information, feel free to discuss these with your physician at your next visit or contact our offices so that we may update your record.

Our goal in expanding your health information available through YourHealth Online is to help you improve your health.   Thank you for your understanding as we add new features to YourHealth Online.

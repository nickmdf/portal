Step 1 of 2: Choose prescriptions to renew

Your YourHealth Online request to have a new updated (renewal) prescription written will be sent to your Clinician's office.  Please submit a request if you have 0 refills on a medication.

The prescription must be approved by your provider.  Once approved, it will be sent to your pharmacy within 2 business days. Please contact your pharmacy prior to picking up your medication to be sure it is ready.

Important note: Please do not submit a YourHealth Online Rx Renewal Request if you simply need a medication refilled by your pharmacy.  Instead you must contact your pharmacy directly to have your prescription refilled.
